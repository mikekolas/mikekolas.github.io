Escape Room

In this page there are various automations that are used currently at an Escape Room. More devices and videos to be added soon.

Rotary Phone

The Rotary Phone is an old hacked dial phone able to play recorded tracks from an SD card when you dial specific telephone numbers found in the room. The Arduino is able to calculate pulses from the dial and decide the corresponding number to be saved as an input. The device comprises of the phone body, the rotary dial, an Arduino UNO, a card reader and SD card.

Laser room

Remember all this old heist or secret agent movies that required accuracy and precision? Well Laser Room is exactly that. This Room has the central panel that you can select difficulty pressing the colored arcade buttons - how many "lives" you have - and each laser is checked through sensors if it is cutted at the end points. In order to deactivate the system you must push the mushroom switch at the end of the run. The system comprises of the central panel, the sensors, the mushroom Trap, the lasers and Arduino UNO.
